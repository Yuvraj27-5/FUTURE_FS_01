How FolioHub Hire Works

Find the perfect creative talent for your project in 3 simple steps.

1
Browse & Filter
Use our powerful filters — location, skills, work type, availability — to find exactly the talent you need. Browse portfolios and projects to see real work.
2
Send an Inquiry
Click "Send Inquiry" on any freelancer's profile to send them a message. Describe your project, timeline, and budget. Freelancers typically respond within 24 hours.
3
Collaborate & Ship
Agree on terms, collaborate through FolioHub messaging, and deliver your project. Rate the experience to help others in the community.
💡 Pro Tip
Post a job to receive applications from multiple qualified freelancers. Describe your project clearly and include your budget range for faster matching.
